## Featured snippet opportunities

**Q: "Does Connexx integrate with Shopify?"**
**Model answer:** Yes. Connexx connects to Shopify in under 10 minutes via the official app. Orders flow into the multi-carrier dashboard automatically. Each order is rate-checked across every active UK and international carrier, the cheapest compliant option is selected, and the label is generated in one click. No manual export, no CSV upload.

**Q: "How does multi-carrier shipping work for eCommerce?"**
**Model answer:** Multi-carrier shipping lets one eCommerce store use Royal Mail, DPD, Evri, Parcelforce, Amazon Shipping, DHL and others from a single platform. When a Shopify or WooCommerce order is created, the system rate-shops across every active carrier, applies your dispatch rules (cost, speed, weight, destination), and prints the label for the optimal carrier. Connexx does this in under 200ms per order.

**Q: "Will I save money switching from one carrier to multi-carrier?"**
**Model answer:** Yes, in most cases. Peak Commerce cut shipping costs 42% in its first quarter on Connexx by switching from a single carrier to multi-carrier rate shopping across 8 providers. Savings come from picking the right carrier per shipment (Royal Mail for letter-box, Evri for sub-2kg, DPD for next-day heavy) instead of forcing every parcel through one carrier's rate card.

**Q: "How do I ship from Shopify to the EU after Brexit?"**
**Model answer:** Three things: register for IOSS if you sell consignments under €150 to EU consumers, get an EORI number, and use DDP Incoterms so your customer doesn't get hit with surprise import VAT. Connexx auto-generates the commercial invoice, IOSS number and HS codes for every EU shipment from Shopify, and routes through DHL Express, DPD, Evri EU or InPost based on destination.
