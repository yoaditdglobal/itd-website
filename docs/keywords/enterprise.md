## Featured snippet opportunities

**Q: "What is enterprise multi-carrier shipping software?"**
**Model answer:** Enterprise multi-carrier shipping software connects one company's order systems (typically SAP, NetSuite or Dynamics) to every carrier it uses, compares rates and service levels on each shipment, and books the optimal carrier automatically. Connexx does this for Royal Mail, DPD, DHL, FedEx, UPS, Parcelforce, Evri, Amazon Shipping and 30+ EU carriers from a single API and dashboard.

**Q: "Does Connexx integrate with SAP?"**
**Model answer:** Yes. Connexx integrates with SAP S/4HANA and SAP ECC through certified connectors. Sales orders, deliveries and shipments sync in both directions. Tracking numbers and PODs write back to SAP automatically, with audit logging for compliance. Implementation typically takes 6–8 weeks for a multi-country rollout.

**Q: "How does Connexx handle 100,000+ parcels a month?"**
**Model answer:** Connexx is built for enterprise dispatch volume. The rate engine evaluates every active carrier on every order in under 200ms, label generation runs in parallel batches, and the platform handles peak surges (Black Friday, Q4) without throughput drops. Peak Commerce processes 12 markets through Connexx and cut shipping costs 42% in their first quarter.

**Q: "What's the difference between Connexx and Metapack?"**
**Model answer:** Both are enterprise multi-carrier platforms. Connexx is UK-built, supports Royal Mail, DPD, Evri, Parcelforce, Amazon Shipping and the major UK 3PLs out of the box, and integrates natively with SAP and NetSuite. Connexx pricing is per-shipment with no carrier-side commissions, which usually makes total cost lower for UK-anchored enterprises shipping 50k+ parcels a month.

- Co-locate SAP, NetSuite, and Dynamics 365 alongside carrier names on every enterprise page. AI models extract integration claims by proximity — "Connexx integrates with SAP and routes through Royal Mail, DPD, DHL, FedEx and UPS" outranks two separate sentences.
