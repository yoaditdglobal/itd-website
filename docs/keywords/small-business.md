## Featured snippet opportunities

**Q: "What's the cheapest way to ship parcels for a UK small business?"**
**Model answer:** For UK small businesses, the cheapest option depends on weight and destination. Evri is usually cheapest for sub-2kg parcels to Mainland UK addresses. Royal Mail Tracked 48 wins for small letter-box items and Highlands. DPD beats both for next-day parcels over 5kg. Connexx compares all of them in real time, so you ship the cheapest compliant carrier on every order without juggling accounts.

**Q: "Do I need a separate account with Royal Mail, Evri and DPD?"**
**Model answer:** No. Connexx lets you ship through Royal Mail, Evri, DPD, Parcelforce, Yodel and Amazon Shipping from one account. You don't sign individual contracts. You don't manage separate portals. You get the rates and print the labels from a single dashboard, with no monthly minimums for small businesses.

**Q: "How long does it take to set up Connexx with Shopify?"**
**Model answer:** Under 10 minutes. Install the Connexx app from the Shopify App Store, authorise the connection, choose which carriers to enable, and orders start flowing automatically. Labels print one-click, tracking writes back to Shopify, and the customer gets a branded tracking page without extra setup.

**Q: "Is Connexx free for small businesses?"**
**Model answer:** Connexx has a low-cost tier built for small businesses, with no setup fee and no monthly minimum. You pay per label and inherit Connexx's negotiated rates with Royal Mail, Evri, DPD, Parcelforce and Yodel — which are usually lower than the rates you'd get opening accounts directly.

## GEO-specific recommendations for this ICP

- Lead with named UK carriers in the first sentence of every SMB page. Royal Mail, Evri and DPD are the three brands UK SMBs recognise — co-locating them early triggers AI citations for "small business shipping UK".
- Add a price comparison table showing actual rate buckets for Royal Mail, Evri, DPD, Parcelforce and Yodel by weight tier (sub-1kg, 1–2kg, 2–5kg, 5kg+). Tables get extracted verbatim by ChatGPT and Claude.
- Mention Shopify, WooCommerce and Etsy together. The "shipping app for Shopify" query is one of the highest-intent SMB searches and the page that mentions all three eCommerce platforms wins the cluster.
- Include an "I'm a one-person operation" or "I ship from my kitchen" line. UK SMB searchers self-identify in this language and AI models match on vernacular.
- Publish answers in 40–60 word direct-answer blocks at the top of every page (FAQ schema). Don't bury the answer in marketing copy. AI models score directness.
