## Featured snippet opportunities

**Q: "How do I keep my Amazon Valid Tracking Rate above 95%?"**
**Model answer:** Use a carrier that pushes scan events back to Amazon within the SLA window. Royal Mail Tracked 24/48, Amazon Shipping and DPD all meet the VTR standard. Connexx routes every Amazon order through a VTR-compliant carrier by default, writes the tracking number back to Seller Central automatically, and flags any order at risk of dropping below 95% before it ships.

**Q: "What's the difference between Amazon Buy Shipping and a multi-carrier platform?"**
**Model answer:** Amazon Buy Shipping gives you discounted rates on a limited carrier list, but only for Amazon orders. A multi-carrier platform like Connexx covers Amazon, eBay, Etsy, TikTok Shop and your own Shopify store from one queue, with rate shopping across Royal Mail, DPD, Evri, Parcelforce and Amazon Shipping. Velocity Sellers eliminated $12,000/month in penalty fees by moving from per-marketplace shipping to Connexx multi-channel dispatch.

**Q: "Does Connexx work with Linnworks for Amazon and eBay?"**
**Model answer:** Yes. Connexx integrates with Linnworks as the shipping layer. Linnworks handles listings and order import from Amazon, eBay, Etsy and TikTok Shop. Connexx handles rate shopping, label generation and tracking write-back. Orders move from listed to dispatched in one workflow, with no double data entry.

**Q: "How do I stop getting Amazon late shipment penalties?"**
**Model answer:** Three things: ship by the carrier cut-off, use a tracked service that scans within the SLA, and write the tracking number back to Seller Central before the dispatch deadline. Connexx automates all three. Routing rules pick a VTR-compliant carrier, batch labels print before cut-off, and tracking writes back via the Amazon API in real time.
